About the app

As an avid NBA fan I have always been interested in comparing players. We will never know what it would have been like for the 2015 Golden State Warriors to play against the 1995 Chicago bulls but we can see how their stats stack up to each other. This simple shiny app is my attempt at doing that.

How it works

Choose a player in the NBA (2015 season), then the algorithm that I created finds the 10 most comparable players (past and present) and uses their stats to predict what the selected players next few seasons will look like. The data was collected from basketball-reference.com and is per 100 possesion data.
